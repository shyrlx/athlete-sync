Smart Travel Buffers Enabled

Athlete-Sync now pulls live traffic and transit data from your current location to all upcoming event venues. Buffer blocks are automatically inserted 45, 30, and 15 minutes before departure — fully adjustable per sport type.

  • → Real-time traffic API integration
  • → Multi-modal transit support (driving, cycling, public transport)

Apple Calendar & iCal Native Integration

iCloud and iCal feeds now sync bidirectionally with Athlete-Sync in under 5 seconds. Events written from your Apple device appear instantly in your Athlete-Sync timeline — and vice versa — without manual refresh.

  • → Sub-5s bidirectional sync

  • → iOS Shortcut automation hooks
