Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Last updated: May 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an Athlete-Sync account or use our services, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Your full name, email address, password (stored securely using industry-standard encryption), and role selection (athlete, coach, or team manager).
  • Scheduling Data: Calendar events, training sessions, match schedules, and venue preferences you input or sync through our platform.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including pages visited, features used, and session duration.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and IP address for security and optimization purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information exclusively to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Athlete-Sync scheduling platform.
  • Process your account registration and manage your user profile.
  • Deliver AI-powered scheduling features including conflict resolution and travel window calculations.
  • Send you important service-related communications, updates, and security alerts.
  • Respond to your support requests and provide customer service.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest.
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Strict access controls and authentication protocols for our internal systems.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent (e.g., sharing schedules with your coach or teammates).
  • With service providers who help us operate our platform, under strict data protection agreements.
  • When required by law, subpoena, or government regulation.

5. Cookies & Tracking

Athlete-Sync uses essential cookies to maintain your login session and preferences. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. You can manage cookie settings through your browser preferences at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct — update or correct inaccurate information in your profile.
  • Delete — request permanent deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Export — download your scheduling data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw Consent — opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will permanently remove your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Athlete-Sync is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
